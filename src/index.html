HOW IT WORKS

1

MEET YOUR COUCH

Have an intro call to meet your coach and learn about their approach. Meanwhile, your coach gets to know more about you, your goals and what you’re hoping to achieve.

2

GET STARTED

Download our Wizyu App and start tracking your daily food intake without any prior guidance for the first 7 days. This allows for your coach to create suggestions. Don’t worry, we’ve made recording your meals easy. Simply download the Wizyu App and take a photo of each meal - no measuring, no listing each ingredient. Just a quick photo with a brief description.

3

ON-BOARDING

After this observation week, you’ll have a follow-up call with your coach. They will provide you with insight on what they’ve observed, and what you can do to improve,

4

START LOSING

With your coach at your side, you’ll continue to record your daily intake and connect with your coach in the Wizyu App. Throughout the program, your coach will give you real-time suggestions and feedback. So if you’re curious about portion sizes or choices, your coach is there at the touch of a button.
